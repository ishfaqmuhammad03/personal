BIOGRAPHY

Dr. Muhammad Ishfaq is an Associate Professor in the college of Computer Science. His research areas of interest are In silico pharmacology, Machine learning, Computational Pharmacology and Toxicology, Drug targets, QSAR/QSPR modelling, and Cheminformatics. Dr. ISHFAQ has published several research articles in various prestigious international journals with good impact factor. He is working as a volunteer reviewer in different prestigious journals. He is currently working on machine learning-based drug discovery and design and focusing on developing cutting edge technology on AI integrating biomedical big data searching for drugs targeting various diseases.
